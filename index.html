About Our Studio

The Academy of Classical and Contemporary Dance (ACCD) has been a cornerstone of the Chesapeake, Virginia dance community for 38 years. As a locally owned and operated studio, we take immense pride in our dedicated instructors, talented students, and the high-quality instruction we provide. Our competitive team, Fusion Dance Company, stands out as one of the premier dance teams in the Mid-Atlantic region. Fusion consistently earns top honors at regional competitions, including awards for best choreography, overall high scores, best costume, and first-place solo performances in both junior and senior divisions. The team has also been recognized for best sportsmanship and professionalism, reflecting the values we instill in our dancers. We offer a wide array of fun and engaging dance classes for all ages and skill levels, including:

  • Ballet
  • Jazz
  • HipHop
  • Tap
  • Contemporary
  • Kinderdance
  • ... and more!

What truly sets ACCD apart is our exceptional team of professional instructors. Our educators are committed to teaching the art of dance, focusing not just on steps and routines, but on cultivating a deep understanding and appreciation for the discipline. This comprehensive approach ensures that our students evolve into well-rounded dancers, many of whom continue their dance journeys at the collegiate level and beyond.Join us at ACCD, where passion meets excellence, and every dancer is empowered to shine.

Meet our Director

Lynne Economou has been the Owner and Artistic Director of the Academy of Classical and Contemporary Dance (ACCD) for over 30 years. She earned her Bachelor of Arts in Dance & Theatre from Old Dominion University, where she was a founding member of the University Dance Theatre. Her advanced training includes intensive studies at the Joffrey Ballet School and Broadway Dance Center in New York City. As Director of Fusion Dance Company, ACCD's competitive team, Lynne has led the group to numerous accolades in the Hampton Roads area. Fusion has been recognized with awards such as Best Technique, Top Studio, Most Professional, and was recently nominated for an Industry Dance Award in the Musical Theatre category. Lynne's mission has always been to provide students of all levels with exceptional dance education in a healthy, structured environment. Her commitment to nurturing talent and fostering discipline has helped countless dancers thrive both on and off the stage.

Fusion Dance Team

Fusion Dance Company is the competitive performance team of our Academy, with dancers selected each year through auditions. The team competes regularly and has earned numerous accolades, including Best Choreography, Overall High Score of the Day, Best Costume, First Place in both the Junior and Senior Solo Divisions, Best Sportsmanship, and the Most Professional Award. Many of our routines have also received the highest point value honors.
